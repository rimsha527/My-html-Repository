Masala Maggi

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Maggi noddles
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp oil/butter
  • 1/2 onion(chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 green chilli(optional)
  • Maggi masala
  • pinch of salt & chilli powder

Instructions

  • Heat oil in a pan
  • Add chopped onions ,saute till golden
  • Add tomato & chilli
  • Pour water and let it boil
  • Add tastemaker+ extra salt/chilli powder
  • Break maggi noddles and add water
  • cook for 2-3 mn until soft and mixwd well
  • Serve hot
